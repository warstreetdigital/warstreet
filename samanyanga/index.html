IN MEMORY OF
PETER C KAZUNGU
1938 — 2026
SAMANYANGA
He walked his years with purpose.

From February 16, 1938
to January 07, 2026,
his life touched many paths.

He was a father, a guide,
and a name that will continue
through those who remember him.

We do not mark his absence —
we honor the life he completed.
A memory preserved by Warstreet Experience Model
